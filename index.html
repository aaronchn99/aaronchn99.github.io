Pong

A simple single player paddle game, based on the early Pong games in the 1970s. Use the Up/Down (Or W/S keys if you are an avid PC Gamer) to control your paddle on the left to compete with the AI opponent, aim to shoot the ball past the opponent's paddle, first to 10 points win.

My first game developed using Godot, where I leveraged my experience with Unity to get familiar with the game engine. In implementing the opponent, I experimented with different AI techniques, tweaking with various handicap techniques so to give the player a chance.
